About Us

Convergency is a research group dedicated to using robotics and artificial intelligence to address real-world problems and making advanced research accessible to the general public. We aim to speed up adoption and implementation of emerging technologies in daily lives and democratize understanding of deep tech topics. We're run by students, for students - we move fast, break things, and are willing to take anyone on given the crucial ingredient of passion.
