Modus Operandi

We're by students, for students. Profit has never been a priority for us - we take revel in our results and take pride in our ability to serve our community.

We do projects. Bureaucracy is boring - we prefer building things and savoring the fruits of our labor.

We move fast and break things. Our team has no one designated leader - members are encouraged to take initiative themselves, collectively working towards our end goal of application of novel research and democratization of STEM. New ideas, constant brainstorming, and communication are encouraged.

We work for the public. Our ideas and projects center around real-world applications, with use cases for the underprivileged and marginalized communities placed at the forefront of our ideation discussions and engineering process.
